Tale of a Norwegian startup

Gressquel was founded in 2015 and is based in Oslo. In addition to various public projects in consumer market we also provide highly specialized expertise in areas such as:

  • GDPR, encryption and information security
  • Development of Microsoft addons (Outlook, Sharepoint, Skype4B, Windows, Excel etc.)
  • Implementation of payment solutions such as Vipps, Nets and Stripe.
  • Swift development.
